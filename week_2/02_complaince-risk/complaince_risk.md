# Quantitative Risk Assessment: Ransomware Scenario

### 📊 ALE Calculation
| Asset | Threat | Single Loss Expectancy (SLE) | Annual Rate of Occurrence (ARO) | Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Customer Database | Ransomware | $10,000 (Data Recovery + Downtime) | 0.2 (Once every 5 years) | **$2,000** |

### 🛡️ Risk Treatment Plan
- **Risk Level:** Medium (Likelihood: 2, Impact: 4)
- **Treatment:** Mitigation
- **Control:** Implement Offline Backups (ISO A.12.3) and Endpoint Detection (NIST Protect).
- **Residual Risk:** Low (Post-control ARO reduced to 0.05).
